### 🚀 How to See Your New Icon

**In Browser:**
1. Restart Django server (if running)
2. Visit http://127.0.0.1:8000/
3. Check browser tab - you'll see the new favicon!

**On Android (after deployment):**
1. Deploy your app to a server
2. Open in Chrome on Android
3. Add to Home Screen
4. Your unique icon appears on home screen!

**Test in DevTools:**
1. Open Chrome DevTools (F12)
2. Go to "Application" tab
3. Click "Manifest" in left sidebar
4. See your icons displayed
