📖 Quick Start Guide

Step 1: Register Your Company

1 Click "Register Company" on the home page

2 Fill in company details (name, email, phone, address)

3 Create username and password

4 Click "Register" - you're now logged in!

Step 2: Create Employee Accounts

1 From company dashboard, click "Register Employee"

2 Choose role: Manager, Supervisor, or Operator

3 Fill in employee details

4 Employees can now login with their credentials

Step 3: Set Up Materials

1 Go to "Materials" in the menu

2 Add material types (virgin, regrind, mixed)

3 Add masterbatch colors with codes

4 Create material recipes for standard formulations

Step 4: Create Production Order

1 Go to "Production Orders"

2 Click "Create New Order"

3 Fill in product, customer, quantity, due date

4 Select material recipe - app calculates requirements!

5 Set priority and save

Step 5: Start Production

1 From dashboard, click "Start New Run"

2 Select mould and machine

3 Enter setter information

4 Run appears on dashboard - track duration

5 Click "Stop Run" when finished

Step 6: Record Shift Production

1 Go to "Shift Production"

2 Click "Record Production"

3 Select order, shift, and enter quantities

4 App calculates scrap rate and efficiency

5 Supervisor approves - updates order total

🎯 Feature Guides

📊 Dashboard

Purpose: Central hub showing all key metrics

What you see:

  • Active moulds count
  • Pending orders with priorities
  • Open issues with urgency levels
  • Housekeeping status
  • Active mould runs with duration
  • Quick action buttons

🏭 Mould Runs

Purpose: Track which moulds are currently running

How to use:

  • Click "Start New Run" from dashboard
  • Select mould and machine number
  • Enter setter name and times
  • App tracks duration automatically
  • Stop run when complete

📦 Production Orders

Purpose: Manage customer orders from start to finish

Features:

  • Priority levels (Urgent, High, Normal, Low)
  • Due date tracking with overdue warnings
  • Automatic material calculations
  • Status tracking (Pending, In Progress, Completed)
  • Shift production summary

🎨 Material Management

Purpose: Track inventory and formulations

Components:

  • Material Types: Virgin, regrind, mixed materials
  • Masterbatches: Colors and additives with codes
  • Recipes: Standard formulations for products
  • Stock Tracking: Current stock with low stock alerts

⚠️ Issue Tracking

Purpose: Log and resolve problems

Issue Types:

  • Customer complaints
  • Mould issues
  • Product defects
  • Machine problems
  • Maintenance needs

Features: Priority levels, time tracking, comments, job cards

🔧 Troubleshooting Database

Purpose: Quick reference for common problems

Includes:

  • 17 detailed troubleshooting guides
  • 10 common defect types
  • Step-by-step solutions

✅ Hourly Checklists

Purpose: Regular quality checks

How to use:

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I reset my password?

A: Contact your company administrator to reset your password.

Q: Can I edit a production order after creating it?

A: Yes, go to Production Orders, click on the order, and use the edit option.

Q: What happens if I forget to stop a mould run?

A: You can stop it anytime from the dashboard. The duration will be calculated from start to stop time.

Q: How do I know if materials are running low?

A: The Material Dashboard shows low stock alerts when stock falls below minimum levels.

Q: Can I access the app on my phone?

A: Yes! The app is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and computers.
